Project information

  • Category: Single Residential
  • Project date: October 2020
  • Location: Hawane

Project Description

Project description: A contemporary farmhouse on an open landscape in Hawane, designed to harmonize the modern and traditional architectural concepts. The design features a double garage, guest toilet, open-plan living, dining, kitchen areas, two bedrooms sharing a bathroom and master bedroom within closet and ensuit. Most spaces celebrate the country views.
