Project Description

Rooted into the sloped landscape of the Nkonyeni Golf Estate, house BEM commands impressive south facing views. The site is located in a humid subtropical climatic region, Manzini, severely dominated by a dense group of shrubs and trees. Designed to stand over and above the dense group of shrubs and trees, seeking attention from a distance and the utilization of concrete roofs and huge openings.
