Project Description

#analysis .....got to site the first time... couldn't move from point to point without having to go around trees/shrubs, and that was a bit frustrating.....yet the client wanted us to keep as much trees as possible.
#concept .....creating an axis that connects the different spaces....thus giving the client this one time experience of moving around (within the site) without any disturbances upfront.
#execution .....first imagined the axis cutting through the thicket....and the trees/shrubs being the different spaces in their irregular arrangements and different sizes.
#happyclient ....this idea allows the structure to be part of the thicket....and promises to give the client a whole new experience immediately from the doorstep.
