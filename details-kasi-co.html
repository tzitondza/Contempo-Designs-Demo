Project information

  • Category: Developments
  • Client: Kasi-Co
  • Project date: 18 October, 2021
  • Location: Ezwulwini, Mvutjini

Details

A contemporary diner proposal located at Mvutjini, a small area at the edge of the Ezulwini suburban context. With Mvutjini being a peri-urban area and Ezulwini proving to be a high-earning suburban area, the design ought to accommodate both lifestyles to the development, hence mixing two architectural languages to come up with an ambiguous master piece. Different types of construction methods and materials will be used to execute this concept; these include colour coated IBR cladding, brick and mortar, timber pallets, wooden decking, reinforced concrete slab, steel columns, aluminum and glass.
