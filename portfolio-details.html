Project information

  • Category: Commercial
  • Client: MPD Mixed Used Development
  • Project date: July 2019
  • Location: Nhlangano

Project Description

The main concept is to create a sense of welcom, arrival and place by the use of reddish woody texture which is common in the area. The 2 blocks widen towards the main access road for a sense of welcome. Bit of fluidity is introduced into the design getting rid of the cubic tradition in the area giving back to the public something new and exciting.
